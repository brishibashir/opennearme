About This Place

The Evening Cup is a cozy corner where time slows down and every sip tells a story. Nestled in the heart of New York, we welcome you with warm lighting, gentle music, and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee that wraps around you like a familiar embrace.

Whether you're seeking a quiet moment to yourself, a place to catch up with an old friend, or simply a warm cup to end your day, our doors are always open. We believe that everyone deserves a place to pause, breathe, and feel at home.
