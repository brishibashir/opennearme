A Quiet Corner of the Internet ??

We built OpenNearMe with a simple belief: everyone deserves to find a welcoming place, no matter what hour it is.

Our Story

OpenNearMe began as a small idea between friends who often found themselves wandering quiet streets late at night, wondering: "Where can I go right now?"

We realized that while the world never truly sleeps, finding the places that welcome you at any hour can be surprisingly difficult. So we decided to build a gentle guide — a place where you can discover businesses that are open and ready to serve you, whenever you need them.

Today, OpenNearMe connects thousands of people with the places they love — from cozy cafes and caring pharmacies to friendly neighborhood shops and reliable gas stations. And we're just getting started.
